# Nexus Technologies Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Last Updated:** November 15, 2024

## General Refund Policy

Nexus Technologies offers refunds for FlowSync subscriptions under the following conditions:

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
All new subscriptions are covered by a 30-day money-back guarantee from the date of first payment. If you are not satisfied with FlowSync within the first 30 days, you may request a full refund by contacting support@nexustech.io.

### Monthly Subscriptions
Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time. Upon cancellation:
- Access continues through the end of the current billing period
- No partial-month refunds are issued
- Unused executions do not carry over

### Annual Subscriptions
Annual subscriptions cancelled within the first 30 days receive a full refund. After 30 days:
- Refunds are calculated on a pro-rata basis
- The refund amount is based on the number of full months remaining
- A 10% early termination fee applies
- Refunds are processed within 10 business days

## Enterprise Refund Policy

Enterprise customers have separate refund terms defined in their Master Service Agreement (MSA). Standard enterprise terms include:

### Cancellation with Cause
If Nexus fails to meet the SLA commitments outlined in the MSA for two or more consecutive months, the customer may cancel with 30 days written notice and receive a pro-rata refund for the remaining contract term without an early termination fee.

### Cancellation Without Cause
Enterprise customers may cancel without cause with 90 days written notice. A pro-rata refund is issued for unused service, minus a 15% early termination fee.

### Migration Assistance
When an Enterprise customer cancels, Nexus provides:
- 60 days of continued access for data export
- Data export assistance in standard formats (CSV, JSON)
- A dedicated point of contact during the transition period
- API access maintained during the 60-day export window

## Pro-Rata Calculation

Pro-rata refunds for annual plans are calculated as:

```
Refund = (Annual Price / 12) × Remaining Full Months × 0.9
```

The 0.9 multiplier accounts for the 10% early termination fee.

**Example:** A customer on the Pro plan ($1,788/year) cancels after 7 months:
- Remaining months: 5
- Refund: ($1,788 / 12) × 5 × 0.9 = $670.50

## Exception Handling

### Service Outages
If FlowSync experiences a service outage exceeding the SLA threshold, affected customers receive:
- A service credit equal to 10× the duration of the outage
- An option to cancel and receive a pro-rata refund without the early termination fee

### Defective Features
If a documented feature is materially non-functional and not resolved within 15 business days of reporting:
- Customer may request a refund for the affected billing period
- Annual customers may cancel without the early termination fee

### Duplicate Charges
Duplicate charges are refunded in full within 3 business days of notification. Contact billing@nexustech.io.

## How to Request a Refund

1. **Email** billing@nexustech.io with the subject line "Refund Request — [Your Company Name]"
2. Include your account ID and reason for the request
3. The billing team will respond within 2 business days with next steps
4. Approved refunds are processed according to the timeline above

## Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:
- Professional services (implementation, training, consulting)
- Custom development work
- Marketplace third-party integrations
- Domain registration fees
