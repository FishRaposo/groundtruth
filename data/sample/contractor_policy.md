# Nexus Technologies Contractor Access Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Document Owner:** People Operations and IT Security

## Overview

This policy governs system access, data handling, and operational requirements for all contractors and temporary workers engaged by Nexus Technologies.

## Access Levels

### Level 1 — Basic Access
Available to all contractors by default:
- Company email account (contractor domain)
- Slack (restricted to assigned channels only)
- Zoom (basic license)
- Notion (read-only access to designated spaces)

### Level 2 — Standard Access
Requires manager and IT Security approval:
- All Level 1 tools
- Linear (assigned projects only)
- Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides — shared documents only)
- Staging environment access (read-only)

### Level 3 — Elevated Access
Requires director, IT Security, and Legal approval:
- All Level 2 tools
- Production environment access (read-only with audit logging)
- Customer data systems (limited to assigned accounts)
- API keys (scoped and time-limited)

### Level 4 — Full Access
Requires VP, IT Security, and Legal approval. Limited to long-term contractors (6+ months):
- All Level 3 tools
- Production write access (with change management approval)
- Administrative access to specific systems
- Customer PII access (with DPA on file)

## Tool Access Restrictions

### Prohibited Tools for Contractors
Contractors may NOT access:
- Financial systems (NetSuite, Expensify)
- HR systems (Workday, payroll)
- Security tools (admin consoles, key management)
- Board-level communications
- Other contractors' performance records

### Time-Limited Access
All contractor access is provisioned with an expiration date:
- Short-term contractors (< 3 months): Access expires on contract end date
- Long-term contractors (3-6 months): Access reviewed quarterly
- Extended contractors (6+ months): Access reviewed monthly

Access is automatically revoked at expiration. Extensions require re-approval.

## Data Handling Requirements

### Data Classification
Contractors must follow the Nexus data classification policy:
- **Public** data: No restrictions
- **Internal** data: May access as needed for assigned work
- **Confidential** data: Access only with explicit approval; must not share externally
- **Restricted** data: Access only with Level 3+ approval and DPA on file

### Data Storage
- All work must be performed on company-provided or approved devices
- Customer data must not be downloaded to personal devices
- Local copies of confidential data must be deleted within 24 hours of task completion
- All file sharing must use company-approved tools (Google Workspace, Notion)

### Data Retention
Contractors must return or delete all company data within 5 business days of contract termination. Confirmation of deletion must be provided in writing to it-security@nexustech.io.

## NDA Requirements

All contractors must:
1. Sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before receiving any system access
2. Complete data handling training within the first 48 hours of engagement
3. Acknowledge the Acceptable Use Policy
4. Complete annual security awareness refresher training (for engagements over 6 months)

The NDA remains in effect for 2 years following contract termination.

## Termination Procedures

### Contractor-Initiated Termination
1. Notify the hiring manager with at least 5 business days notice
2. Complete all in-progress work or hand off documentation
3. Submit all deliverables
4. Return company equipment within 5 business days
5. Confirm deletion of all local data copies

### Company-Initiated Termination
1. Hiring manager notifies People Ops and IT Security
2. System access is revoked within 4 hours of notification
3. Company equipment collected within 2 business days
4. Final payment processed according to contract terms
5. Data deletion confirmation required within 5 business days

### Immediate Revocation
System access is revoked immediately (within 1 hour) in cases involving:
- Security policy violations
- Unauthorized data access or sharing
- NDA violations
- Conflicts of interest
- Performance or conduct issues requiring immediate action

## Compliance

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Immediate access revocation
- Contract termination
- Legal action per NDA terms
- Reporting to relevant regulatory bodies if applicable

Questions about this policy should be directed to contractor-policy@nexustech.io.
